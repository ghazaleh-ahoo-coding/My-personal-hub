About Me:

My name is Ghazaleh Azimi, and I’m from the historic city of Herat, Afghanistan. I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree at the American University of Afghanistan (AUAF), where I am deepening my knowledge and skills in both language and technology. With a strong passion for education, communication, and personal growth, I strive to make a meaningful impact through everything I do.

I have a deep love for traveling and adventure, and I enjoy capturing beautiful moments through photography. One of my biggest dreams is to blend my creative side with my technical skills. The first project I plan to create after learning web development is a personal website called Pixel, where I will showcase my recent photos of nature, landscapes, and life’s special moments.

I am particularly drawn to the intersection of education and technology, and I am eager to explore how these fields can intersect and complement each other. Looking ahead, my goal is to become a dynamic professional who bridges the gap between education and technology. I aim to further develop my expertise in web development, project management, and teaching. Whether it’s through empowering students, building digital solutions, or contributing to community projects, I hope to play a key role in shaping a brighter and more connected future.
